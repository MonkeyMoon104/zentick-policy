Privacy Policy for ZenTick

Last Updated: January 7, 2026

Introduction

ZenTick ("we," "our," or "the app") is a Pomodoro timer application designed to help you focus better through time management. This Privacy Policy explains how ZenTick handles your information.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

ZenTick stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Timer Settings: Your preferred focus duration (15, 25, 45, 60 minutes, or custom)
  • Statistics:
    • Number of completed focus sessions
    • Total time focused (in seconds)
    • Current streak count
    • Longest streak count
  • App Preferences:
    • Dark mode setting (on/off)
    • Sound notification setting (on/off)

Data We Do NOT Collect

ZenTick does NOT:

  • Collect any personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • Send any data to external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Require account creation or login
  • Share any information with third parties

How We Use Your Information

All data collected by ZenTick is used exclusively to:

  • Display your focus statistics
  • Remember your timer preferences
  • Provide you with a personalized experience

Your data never leaves your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally using Android DataStore on your device
  • Data persists only on your device and is not backed up to cloud services by default
  • If you uninstall the app, all your data is automatically deleted from your device

Permissions

ZenTick requests the following Android permissions:

  • VIBRATE: (Currently unused, reserved for future features)
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To show you notifications when timer completes
  • WAKE_LOCK: To keep the timer running even when screen is off
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: (Reserved for future background timer functionality)

These permissions are used only for the app's core functionality and nothing else.

Third-Party Services

ZenTick does NOT use any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage services
  • Social media integrations

Children's Privacy

ZenTick does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and the "Last Updated" date will be revised.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is as secure as your device
  • We recommend using device encryption and screen lock
  • Regular device backups are your responsibility

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View your statistics anytime in the app
  • Delete: Uninstall the app to delete all data
  • Control: Modify or reset your data through app settings
